GPU Instanced Mesh-based Shadows

Why?

Working on project for the mobile platforms, brings a ton of hurdles with respect to the capabilities of the hardware and the limits of the systems you can build.
One of these challenges was that you can only have one directional light casting shadows at a time. This was a problem because I wanted to have multiple dynamic objects casting shadows at the same time. After tinkering around the idea and my brilliant designer outright challenging me that I couldn't do it, I decided to take a stab at it with murderous intent.

I am cleaning up some of the code and will be posting a demo soon. Stay tuned!
