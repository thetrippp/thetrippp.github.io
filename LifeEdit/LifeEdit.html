Welcome to LifeEdit

LifeEdit is a visual time-tracking and productivity app designed to help you take control of your day.

What You Can Do

  • Track Your Time: Create time blocks for different activities like Work, Learning, Leisure, Health, Sleep, and Maintenance.
  • Visual Timeline: See your entire day at a glance on a 24-hour timeline.
  • Drag & Resize: Easily move and resize time blocks to adjust your schedule.
  • Google Calendar Sync: Sign in with Google to sync your events to Google Calendar.
  • Undo & Clear: Made a mistake? Use the Undo button or Clear All to start fresh.

Getting Started

  • Click "+ Add slot" to create your first time block.
  • Right-click on any block to change its category or activity.
  • Drag blocks to move them, or drag the right edge to resize.
  • Use the date navigation to plan future days or review past ones.
